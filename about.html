IEEE Research Publications

YOLOv8-Based Model For Precise Corrosion Segmentation

Abstract: This investigation offers a creative method of corrosion detection by utilizing the YOLOv8 object detection algorithm in tandem with the WANDB platform for efficient model training and deployment. By combining the advanced protest locating skills of YOLOv8 with the visualization capability of the Weights & Biases stage, this article offers a thorough and effective setup for video handling tasks, improving the precision of corrosion detection frameworks.

Conference: 2024 ICCIGST Publisher: IEEE View on IEEE Xplore

Discovering Knee Osteoarthritis Using CNN Enhanced with AlexNet

Abstract: This research proposed a novel approach utilizing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) with the AlexNet architecture to enhance the diagnostic accuracy and severity assessment of knee osteoarthritis. The study leverages a large dataset of knee radiographic images, where the CNN model is trained to discern subtle patterns and nuanced information indicative of OA-related structural changes, enabling it to differentiate between varying stages of the disease.

Conference: 2024 5th ICICV Publisher: IEEE View on IEEE Xplore
